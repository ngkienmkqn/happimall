Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Is there a monthly fee to use Happi Mall?

No! Happi Mall is completely free to use. You only pay for products when your customers make a purchase. There are no monthly fees, no setup fees, and no hidden costs.

How long does production take?

Most products are produced within 72 hours. Some specialized items may take longer. Production times are clearly displayed on each product page.

Can I order samples?

Yes! You can order samples at cost price to check product quality before selling to customers. This helps you make informed decisions about which products to offer.

Do you support international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide from our warehouses in the USA, Europe, Australia, and Asia. This ensures faster delivery times and lower shipping costs for your international customers.

What if a customer wants to return a product?

We have a comprehensive returns policy. If there's a quality issue, we'll replace the product at no cost. For other returns, please contact our support team to discuss specific cases.

Can I use my own branding?

Absolutely! All products ship with your branding. We don't include any Happi Mall branding in the packaging, so it appears to come directly from your business.

How do I track orders?

Once an order is shipped, tracking information is automatically synced to your store. You and your customers will receive tracking numbers to monitor delivery progress.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and offer net payment terms for high-volume sellers. Payment is only charged when orders are fulfilled.

Product Management

How to manage your product catalog

Importing Products

Products can be imported to your store with just one click. All product details including images, descriptions, variants, and pricing are preset and ready to customize.

Setting Your Prices

You control the retail price. We show you the base cost, and you set your markup to determine your profit margin. Most sellers use a 2-3x markup.

Customization Options

Many products support customization - customers can add text, upload images, or choose from design templates. These options are automatically configured when you import.
