Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started

Do I need a credit card to sign up?

No! You can create a free account without providing any payment information. You only pay when your customers place orders.

Can I start without an existing store?

Yes! You can sign up first and set up your store later. We'll guide you through the process of creating a store on your chosen platform.

How long does setup take?

Most users complete setup in under 15 minutes. Creating your account takes 2 minutes, connecting your store takes 5 minutes, and importing your first products takes just a few clicks.

Is there a contract or minimum commitment?

No contract, no commitment. You can use Happi Mall for as long as you like and cancel anytime. There are also no minimum order requirements.
