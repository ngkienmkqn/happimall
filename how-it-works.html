How It Works

Three simple methods to start selling with Happi Mall. Choose the approach that fits your business model.

1

Rookie Method

Find → Checkout
Perfect for beginners or testing products. Simply browse, choose, and fulfill orders manually.

1

Find Products

Browse our catalog of 1000+ high-quality custom products. Filter by category, price, or production time.

2

Receive Customer Orders

When a customer places an order on your store, collect their information and product details.

3

Manual Checkout

Submit the order through Happi Mall with your customer's shipping address and customization details.

4

We Handle Production & Shipping

We produce and ship directly to your customer. You keep the profit margin!

2

Standard Method

Find → Sell → Dropship
Automate your fulfillment. Connect your store and let orders sync automatically.

1

Integrate Your Shop

Connect your Shopify, Etsy, WooCommerce, or other ecommerce platform with Happi Mall.

2

Add Products to Your Store

Import products from our catalog to your store. Customize descriptions and set your retail prices.

3

Customer Orders Automatically

When customers order, the order automatically syncs to Happi Mall for fulfillment.

4

Automated Production & Shipping

We handle everything automatically - production, quality control, packaging, and worldwide shipping.

3

Professional Method

Design → Sell → Dropship
Maximum customization. Upload your designs and create unique branded products.

1

Use Mockup Generator

Access our AI-powered design tools. Upload your artwork and apply it to any product in our catalog.

2

Create Custom Products

Design unique products with your brand. Preview mockups in real-time before publishing.

3

Sync & Sell

Push your custom products directly to your connected stores. Set your pricing and start selling.

4

Automated Fulfillment

Orders sync automatically. We print your designs on demand and ship to your customers worldwide.
